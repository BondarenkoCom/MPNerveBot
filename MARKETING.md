# Marketing Strategy

## Core Thesis

We should not market this as "AI for marketplaces".

That positioning is weak and generic.

We should market it as:

"Telegram control bot for WB/Ozon sellers"

And later:

"Your daily seller ops layer in Telegram"

## Fastest Go-To-Market

### 1. Manual Pilot Sales

Goal:
- close 3-5 pilot sellers

Pitch:
- connect your marketplace tokens
- get a Telegram digest and urgent alerts
- save time on routine marketplace checks

Pilot offer:
- discounted first month
- direct support
- quick setup

### 2. Telegram Distribution

Main channel:
- seller chats
- seller channels
- founder/operator chats

Content format:
- practical posts
- screenshots of digest examples
- "5 problems found in one seller account" style teardown
- before/after workflow examples

### 3. OK.ru Traffic Sidecar

Potential side channel:
- use the existing fake OK.ru page only as a traffic funnel, not as the main product home

Good uses:
- repost practical seller tips
- repost alert examples
- publish short "marketplace pain" content
- funnel traffic into Telegram

Bad uses:
- hard spam
- obvious bot behavior
- low-quality repost floods

Automation ideas for OK.ru:
- queue short educational posts
- rotate CTA blocks into Telegram
- test simple daily content series for seller pain

### 4. Lightweight Referral Loop

The product needs a simple spread mechanism:
- one owner invites a manager into the same chat
- one seller forwards the digest screenshot to another seller
- referral gives free time or higher limits

This is the only "viral" path worth respecting.

## Messaging

### Strong Messaging

- Stop checking two marketplace apps all day. Get one Telegram digest.
- Your seller operations layer in Telegram.
- Know what needs attention today before sales slip.
- Reviews, stock, and anomalies in one place.

### Weak Messaging

- AI marketplace assistant
- smart neural agent
- next-gen analytics platform
- all-in-one ecosystem

## Early Channels

Priority order:

1. Telegram seller communities
2. Direct outreach to seller operators
3. OK.ru side traffic
4. Short landing page
5. Later: official partner catalogs and integrations

## First Content Angles

1. "What a seller should check every morning"
2. "3 things sellers miss because dashboards are fragmented"
3. "How unanswered feedback quietly hurts revenue"
4. "What an operations bot should tell you before lunch"

## First Offer

Pilot setup:
- connect marketplace account
- configure digest
- enable stock and feedback alerts
- get Telegram delivery within one day

## Key Rule

We are not trying to look big.

We are trying to look useful.
